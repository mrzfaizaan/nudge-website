Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

1. Overview

Nudge-HUD ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit nudgehud.work or use the Nudge desktop application, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URLs and exit pages
  • Browser type, operating system, and device information
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)

Google Analytics uses first-party cookies to track this data. No personally identifiable information (name, email, postal address) is collected through analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

2.2 Desktop Application

The Nudge desktop application does not collect telemetry, usage data, or diagnostic information. Your task files (.md) are read and written locally on your machine and are never transmitted to our servers. Application settings are stored locally in a state file within your app data directory.

2.3 License Activation

When you activate a license, the desktop application sends your license key and machine hostname to the Lemon Squeezy License API for validation. This data is processed by Lemon Squeezy, not by Nudge-HUD. No license activation data is stored on our servers.

2.4 Payment Processing

All payments are handled by Lemon Squeezy, our third-party payment processor. When you purchase a license, Lemon Squeezy collects billing information including name, email address, and payment details. We do not receive or store your full payment information. Lemon Squeezy's use of your data is governed by their own Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

The website analytics data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website
  • Improve the website experience and content
  • Monitor site performance and availability

We do not sell, trade, or rent any collected data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data with the following third-party services only to the extent necessary to operate our business:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic and usage analytics
  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and license management

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

5. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained according to the default settings of Google Analytics (currently 26 months). We do not maintain any separate databases or logs containing visitor data.

6. Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies on the nudgehud.work website. Google Analytics sets its own cookies as part of its tracking service, which are governed by Google's cookie policy.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@nudgehud.work.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the website or application after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, reach us at support@nudgehud.work.
