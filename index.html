The Engine.

Every line of your notes is classified into one of three types. You never think about syntax — Nudge classifies your input and applies the right format automatically.

You type → Nudge makes it
# Heading → # Heading
## Sub heading → ## Sub heading
- [ ] Buy milk → ☐ Buy milk
- Just plain notes → Just plain notes
* Bullet List → • Bullet List
1. Numbered List → 1. Numbered List
[ ]

Action

Checkbox nodes you can toggle. Click to mark done — the file on disk updates instantly. Your commitments, tracked.

*

Bullet

Reference items that stay visible but can't be toggled. Perfect for notes, context, and reminders you don't want to accidentally mark done.

-

Plain

Passive context. No checkbox, no bullet. For blockers, notes, and ambient information that anchors your workflow.

1.

Numbered List

Ordered sequences for step-by-step procedures. Keeps your execution order intact without manual re-numbering.
